Call for Papers

The 15th International Scientific Conference on Earth Observation Tools in Protected Areas aims to build capacity and generate marine data knowledge in Small Island Developing States (SIDS), LDCs, and African countries.

Conference Topics

  • Monitoring of algal biomass and harmful algal blooms
  • Coastal sediment movements
  • Location of fronts, eddies, and upwelling zones
  • Palaeoecological Research in Ocean Parks
  • MPAs as resources for human health and well-being
  • Endemic species in marine national parks
  • Climate change threats to single-island endemics
  • MPA creation trajectories in SIDS
  • Ecosystem services in biosphere reserves
  • Multi-taxa approaches in ocean ecosystems
